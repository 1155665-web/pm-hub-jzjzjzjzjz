# One-on-One Meeting Template

## Overview

One-on-one (1:1) meetings are regular, private conversations between a manager and team member. They're crucial for building relationships, providing feedback, and supporting career development.

## 1:1 Best Practices

### DO:
‚úÖ Keep them consistent (don't cancel)  
‚úÖ Let team member set agenda  
‚úÖ Listen more than you talk  
‚úÖ Be present (close laptop)  
‚úÖ Take notes and follow up  
‚úÖ Make it about them, not you  
‚úÖ Create psychological safety

### DON'T:

## Question Bank

### Opening Questions
- "How are you, really?"
- "What's on your mind?"
- "What would make this week/sprint better?"
- "What's the most important thing we should discuss?"

### Current Work Questions
- "What's going well?"
- "What's challenging?"
- "Where are you stuck?"
- "What help do you need from me?"
- "What's energizing you right now?"
- "What's draining your energy?"

### Career Development Questions
- "Where do you see yourself in 6 months? 2 years?"
- "What skills do you want to develop?"
- "What kind of work lights you up?"
- "What would you like to do more of? Less of?"
- "What's one thing you learned recently?"
- "How can I help you grow?"

### Feedback Questions
- "What feedback do you have for me?"
- "How can I better support you?"
- "What's working well in how we work together?"
- "What should I start/stop/continue doing?"
- "Is there anything I should know that I don't?"

### Team & Culture Questions
- "How are things with the team?"
- "Do you feel heard and valued?"
- "What would make our team even better?"
- "Is there anyone you'd like to work with more?"
- "How's the work-life balance?"

## Conversation Frameworks

### GROW Model (Coaching)

**Goal:** What do you want to achieve?  
**Reality:** What's the current situation?  
**Options:** What could you do?  
**Will:** What will you do?

### Start, Stop, Continue

**Start:** What should you start doing?  
**Stop:** What should you stop doing?  
**Continue:** What's working that you should keep doing?

### Situation, Behavior, Impact (Feedback)

**Situation:** When [specific situation]  
**Behavior:** You did [observed behavior]  
**Impact:** The impact was [result]

## Special Situations

### Performance Issues
1. Be direct but compassionate
2. Provide specific examples
3. Discuss impact
4. Create improvement plan together
5. Set clear expectations
6. Offer support
7. Schedule more frequent check-ins

### Career Progression Discussion
1. Review current performance
2. Discuss career aspirations
3. Identify skill gaps
4. Create development plan
5. Define success criteria
6. Set timeline and milestones
7. Regular progress reviews

### Burnout Signs
- Decreased quality or productivity
- Lack of enthusiasm
- Increased cynicism
- Physical/emotional exhaustion
- Withdrawal from team
- More mistakes than usual

**How to help:**
- Acknowledge and validate
- Reduce workload temporarily
- Encourage time off
- Identify root causes
- Create recovery plan
- Check in more frequently

## Remote 1:1 Tips

**Video Call Best Practices:**
- Cameras on
- Good lighting and audio
- Quiet, private space
- Minimize distractions
- Share screen for notes (if helpful)

**Tools:**
- Video: Zoom, Google Meet, Teams
- Notes: Shared Google Doc, Notion
- Actions: Jira, Trello, Asana

**Challenges & Solutions:**
- **Less casual interaction:** Schedule virtual coffee chats
- **Harder to read body language:** Be more explicit, check in more
- **Zoom fatigue:** Offer walk & talk phone option
- **Time zones:** Be flexible, rotate times

## 1:1 Checklist

**Before Meeting:**
- [ ] Review notes from last meeting
- [ ] Check action items status
- [ ] Review team member's recent work
- [ ] Prepare feedback (positive and constructive)
- [ ] Block time in calendar (don't overschedule)

**During Meeting:**
- [ ] Create welcoming atmosphere
- [ ] Let them lead agenda
- [ ] Listen actively
- [ ] Take notes
- [ ] Give and receive feedback
- [ ] Discuss career development
- [ ] Document action items

**After Meeting:**
- [ ] Send summary with action items
- [ ] Follow up on commitments
- [ ] Update tracking doc
- [ ] Set next meeting date
- [ ] Take any immediate actions
