# Daily Standup Template

## What is a Daily Standup?

A brief daily meeting (15 minutes max) where the team synchronizes on progress, plans, and blockers.

---

## Meeting Information

**Time:** [Time] (same time every day)  
**Duration:** 15 minutes maximum  
**Location:** [Room/Video link]  
**Attendees:** Development team, Product Manager, Scrum Master

---

## Format

Each team member answers three questions:

### 1. What did I do yesterday?
*Focus on completed work, not tasks in progress*

### 2. What will I do today?
*Commit to specific deliverables*

### 3. Are there any blockers?
*Anything preventing progress*

## Common Standup Patterns

### Round Robin
- Go around in order
- Everyone gets equal time
- Simple and predictable

### Walking the Board
- Start with stories closest to done
- Focus on completing work
- Team discusses each story

### Yesterday/Today/Blockers
- Standard three questions
- Most common format
- Clear structure

---

## Handling Common Issues

### Issue: Standup runs too long
**Solutions:**
- Set a timer
- Park discussions for after
- Walking the board instead of round robin
- Scrum master enforces time box

### Issue: Team member rambling
**Solutions:**
- Gentle time reminder
- Focus on outcomes, not tasks
- "Let's take that offline"
- One-on-one coaching

### Issue: No blockers ever mentioned
**Solutions:**
- Create safe environment
- Ask directly: "What's slowing you down?"
- Follow up one-on-one
- Look for hidden blockers

### Issue: Feels like status report to PM/manager
**Solutions:**
- Emphasize it's for the team
- PM stays quiet, just listens
- Team members face each other, not PM
- Change facilitation

### Issue: Remote team engagement
**Solutions:**
- Use video (cameras on)
- Rotate who goes first
- Use virtual board
- Keep energy high

## Virtual Standup Tips

**Technology:**
- Video calls (cameras on)
- Screen share sprint board
- Use chat for parking lot items
- Record for async team members

**Engagement:**
- Start with quick personal check-in
- Use reactions/emojis
- Rotate facilitator
- Keep it interactive

**Async Options:**
- Written updates in Slack
- Video recording updates
- Thread-based updates
- Still have live standup 2-3x/week

## Standup Variations

### 15-Minute Rule
If standup goes over 15 min, move remaining people to after-standup discussion

### Blocker-First
Start by asking "Any blockers?" so PM can start resolving immediately

### Focus Days
Monday: Sprint goal alignment  
Wednesday: Mid-sprint check  
Friday: Week wrap-up

### Themed Days
- Monday: What's exciting this week?
- Friday: What did we learn?

- [ ] Spoke clearly
- [ ] Noted action items

**After Standup:**
- [ ] Updated board
- [ ] Followed up on blockers
- [ ] Had parking lot discussions
- [ ] Documented decisions

## Metrics to Track

**Standup Health:**
- Duration: Track daily, target < 15 min
- Attendance: % of team present
- Blockers mentioned: Track trends
- Actions generated: Are blockers being resolved?

**Team Engagement:**
- Participation: Is everyone contributing?
- Energy level: Rate 1-5
- Value: Team survey monthly
