## Retrospective Activities & Formats

### Quick Activities (15-20 min)

**1. One Word Check-in**
Each person shares one word describing their sprint

**2. Emoji Reactions**
Vote with emojis on how the sprint went:
- üéâ Great
- üòä Good
- üòê Okay
- üòï Could be better
- üòû Needs improvement

**3. Temperature Reading**
Rate the sprint from ü•∂ Cold to üî• Hot

### Longer Activities (30-45 min)

**4. Timeline Retrospective**
- Draw sprint timeline on board
- Plot events (good and bad)
- Discuss patterns

**5. Sailboat Retrospective**
- Goal: Island in distance
- Wind: What helped us move forward
- Anchors: What held us back
- Rocks: Risks ahead

**6. 4 Ls**
- Liked: What we enjoyed
- Learned: New insights
- Lacked: What was missing
- Longed for: What we wish for

**7. Speed Car**
- Engine: What drives us forward
- Parachute: What slows us down
- Steering wheel: Who has control
- Road ahead: Opportunities/risks

## Retrospective Best Practices

### DO:
‚úÖ Create safe environment for honesty  
‚úÖ Focus on actions, not just discussion  
‚úÖ Make it different/fun each time  
‚úÖ Time-box discussions  
‚úÖ Follow up on action items  
‚úÖ Rotate facilitator  
‚úÖ Include everyone  

### DON'T:
‚ùå Skip retrospectives  
‚ùå Let one person dominate  
‚ùå Just complain without solutions  
‚ùå Create too many action items  
‚ùå Blame individuals  
‚ùå Use same format every time  
‚ùå Skip follow-up on previous actions

## Retrospective Prime Directive

*"Regardless of what we discover, we understand and truly believe that everyone did the best job they could, given what they knew at the time, their skills and abilities, the resources available, and the situation at hand."*

Start every retrospective by reading this together to set the tone.
