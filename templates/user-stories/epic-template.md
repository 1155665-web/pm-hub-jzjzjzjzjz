## Example: E-commerce Checkout Epic

### Epic Name
Streamlined Guest Checkout Experience

### Epic Summary
**As a** first-time shopper  
**I want** to complete my purchase quickly without creating an account  
**So that** I don't abandon my cart due to registration friction

### Business Context

**Problem Statement:**
Currently, users must create an account to checkout, causing 40% cart abandonment. Market research shows 60% of users prefer guest checkout. We're losing ~$200K/month in potential revenue.

**Opportunity:**
- Reduce cart abandonment by 25%
- Increase conversion rate by 15%
- Improve time-to-purchase by 50%
- Expand accessible market

**Success Criteria:**
- Cart abandonment rate: < 30% (from 40%)
- Guest checkout adoption: > 50% of purchases
- Time to checkout: < 3 minutes average

### User Stories Breakdown

**Phase 1: MVP (Must Have)**
- [ ] As a guest, I can enter shipping info without registration - 8 pts - In Progress
- [ ] As a guest, I can enter payment info securely - 5 pts - Todo
- [ ] As a guest, I receive order confirmation email - 3 pts - Todo
- [ ] As a guest, I can optionally create account after purchase - 5 pts - Todo

**Total: 21 story points (2 sprints)**

### Timeline
**Target Start:** Sprint 24 (Jan 15)  
**Target Completion:** Sprint 26 (Mar 1)  
**Duration:** 6 weeks (3 sprints)

### Milestones
- **Jan 22:** Design approved
- **Feb 5:** MVP in staging
- **Feb 12:** Beta with 10% traffic
- **Mar 1:** Full rollout

### Success Metrics
- **Cart abandonment:** From 40% to 30%
- **Guest checkout usage:** 50% of transactions
- **Checkout time:** From 6 min to 3 min avg
- **Conversion rate:** +15%

### Risks
| Risk | Impact | Probability | Mitigation |
|------|--------|-------------|------------|
| Payment gateway integration issues | High | Medium | Early integration testing, backup gateway |
| Security vulnerabilities | High | Low | Security audit, penetration testing |
| User confusion about guest vs. account | Medium | Medium | Clear UI/UX, user testing |
