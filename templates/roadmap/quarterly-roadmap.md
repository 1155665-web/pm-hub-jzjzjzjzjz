## Tips for Creating Roadmaps

1. **Be realistic**: Don't over-commit
2. **Show themes**: Group by strategic themes
3. **Build flexibility**: Leave 20% capacity for unexpected work
4. **Communicate clearly**: Make sure everyone understands
5. **Update regularly**: Roadmaps are living documents
6. **Focus on outcomes**: What value, not just features
7. **Get buy-in**: Involve stakeholders early
8. **Show dependencies**: Make risks visible
9. **Set expectations**: Be clear about confidence levels
10. **Review often**: Monthly check-ins minimum
